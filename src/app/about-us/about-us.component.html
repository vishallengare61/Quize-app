MMP offers detailed information and the right tools for exam preparation ranging from K-12, Olympiads, NTSE, entrance exams and government recruitment exams. The idea behind this platform is to offer students a single platform for all sorts/categories of examinations.

Our AI-Enabled Make My Path section will help you to set your goal and will recommend best path to achieve the same in minimum possible time. We believe learning always happens in groups, so MMP enables teachers to create groups and share assignments and papers with their students.

Welcome and thank you for visiting MakeMyPath!

Our purpose is to empower every student with the right learning tool. As we wade through the pandemic, online learning platforms have expanded more than ever. However, to leverage the power of technology, students need the right learning tools, personalized as per their needs.
Every child is unique and learns differently. With this in mind, we founded MakeMyPath – India's largest Edu-service platform for various school as well as competitive examinations.
In addition, we didn't leave the mentors behind. An empowered mentor is indispensable for bright and beautiful futures of our students. That's why, we have spent long hours to share the workload of our teachers. Our platform can be used by the mentors to create and customize practice & papers and share the result with students as well as parents.
I am thankful to our entire team members who worked closely to build MakeMyPath - a single AI based platform where students are provided with everything, they need to set their goals and easily find the best path to achieve it.
I am happy to be a part of team that will define the technology powered education of the future.
On behalf of MakeMyPath, I invite and welcome you to explore our website and learn more about everything we have to offer. I am sure whether you are a student (concerned about their future), a parent(who want to provide the best resources to their child) or a mentor (who needs the right tools for their students) – you would love our platform.
